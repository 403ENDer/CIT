About Department of Computing

The Department of Computing, established in 1988, the beginning of the era of IT Revolution to promote Computer Science Education, has evolved into one of the most vibrant departments in CIT with its committed faculty and motivated students.

Vision

Department of Computing endeavors to make the students, world class software engineers, data scientists and decision makers with prudence of pioneering the solutions to the challenges of the nation and the world.

Mission

  • 1. To impart sound conceptual knowledge along with intensive practical training and real-time industry/research project exposure to the students.
  • 2. To provide a learning ambiance to enhance innovation, problem-solving skills, leadership qualities, team-spirit, and ethical responsibilities.
  • 3. To establish an Industry Institute Interaction program to provide exposure to the latest tools and technologies used in IT organizations and enhance entrepreneurship skills.
